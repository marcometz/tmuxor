TMUXor — your tmux Claude Code fleet, hands-free on your glasses. See which session needs you, read its replies, and reply by voice, over your own private Tailscale network.

Setup (you run a small backend on your OWN computer; never share your token — it can run commands on your machine):

1) Get conductor_api.py + tmux_conductor.py from the developer. Set CONDUCTOR_TOKEN + OPENAI_API_KEY, then run: python3 conductor_api.py — and expose it on your tailnet: sudo tailscale serve --bg 8790
2) Install TMUXor, open it, and on the Setup screen tap "Scan QR code" and scan the QR that install.sh printed (or paste the one-line code it shows). No need to ask the developer to whitelist your domain.
3) Use it: tap = do, double-tap = back, swipe = move/scroll. Tap a session to read it (opens at the latest question); tap = talk to reply by voice, review the transcription, tap to send.

Needs: Even G2 glasses, tmux + Claude Code, Tailscale, Python 3.10+, an OpenAI API key.
